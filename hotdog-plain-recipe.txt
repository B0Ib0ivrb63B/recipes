Ingredients:

 - 1 hotdog

 - 1 hotdog bun

 - 1 tbsp ketchup

 - 1 tbsp mustard



Directions:

 1. Preheat water in a saucepan to boiling.

 2. When the water is boiling place the hotdog in and wait until it 'plumps', about 3-4 minutes. 

 3. Take the hotdog out of the water with tongs, put on hotdog bun, ad add ketchup and mustard to taste.
 
 4. Clean-up.
